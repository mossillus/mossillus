About Me

I'm a Data Science and Statistics student at the University of Central Florida with a strong foundation in machine learning, generative AI, and statistical modeling. I apply technical skills in Python, R, SQL, and applied analytics across domains like physics, medicine, business, and higher education to build scalable, reproducible solutions that drive real-world outcomes.

  • Developed LLM-based pipelines to extract structured data from scientific literature in photovoltaics and electronic materials, with outputs in CSV/JSON and visualized evaluation metrics.
  • Led a machine learning project on flight delay prediction using regression, LSTM, time-series, and Random Forest models to evaluate performance trade-offs.
  • Audited 10,000 course materials as a library research assistant and maintained a data portal supporting over $13 million in textbook cost savings.
  • Presented at Open AI and Teaching and Learning with AI as a Student Advocate for the integration of generative AI in higher education.

When I'm not coding, I love to draw, paint and play board games.
